WHAT IS IT?

It's a nifty new web tool, designed to make your election vote count.

On election week 2011, we unveiled the tool and 30,000 Kiwi's showed their support by playing, sharing and sending us some amazingly helpful feedbaaack!

We think that this app has been a really successful aid for heaps of young voters and we've been completely blown-away with the response.

Thanks to your feedback, expect to see a better, faster and smarter version of the tool for the general election in 2014.
